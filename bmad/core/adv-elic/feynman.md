# Feynman Technique

```xml
<elicitation id="bmad/core/adv-elic/feynman.md" name="Feynman Technique">
  <description>Advanced elicitation technique for feynman technique</description>

  <core-method>
    Explain complex concepts in simple terms as if teaching an 8-year-old. Reveals understanding gaps through forced simplification.
  </core-method>

  <llm>
    <action>No jargon allowed (if using technical terms, you don't understand)</action>
    <action>Use analogies and familiar comparisons</action>
    <action>Test with "Why?" and "How?" questions</action>
    <action>Iterate until crystal clear</action>
  </llm>

  <final-report title="Feynman Explanation">
    <value>Concept: [What are you explaining?]</value>
    <value>Child-Level: [8-year-old explanation]</value>
    <value>Analogies: [Real-world comparisons]</value>
    <value>Gaps Found: [Where explanation broke down]</value>
    <value>Simplified: [Even clearer version]</value>
  </final-report>
</elicitation>
```
