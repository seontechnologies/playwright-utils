# Meta-Prompting Analysis

```xml
<elicitation id="bmad/core/adv-elic/meta-prompting.md" name="Meta-Prompting Analysis">
  <description>Advanced elicitation technique for meta-prompting analysis</description>

  <core-method>
    Step outside the current reasoning approach to analyze its structure. Identify assumptions, biases, and limitations in the methodology. Suggest structural improvements to the reasoning process. Test improvements through validation or comparison methods.
  </core-method>

  <llm>
    <action>Examine the current reasoning methodology being used</action>
    <action>Identify unstated assumptions underlying the approach</action>
    <action>Detect potential biases in the reasoning structure</action>
    <action>Highlight limitations or blind spots in current method</action>
    <action>Propose structural improvements to reasoning process</action>
    <action>Suggest validation methods to test the improved approach</action>
  </llm>

  <final-report title="Meta-Analysis of Reasoning Process">
    <value>Current Methodology: [Approach being analyzed]</value>
    <value>Process Strengths: [What works effectively]</value>
    <value>Identified Weaknesses: [Limitations and blind spots]</value>
    <value>Hidden Assumptions: [Unstated premises in reasoning]</value>
    <value>Structural Improvements: [Suggested methodology enhancements]</value>
    <value>Validation Strategy: [How to test improved approach]</value>
  </final-report>
</elicitation>
```
