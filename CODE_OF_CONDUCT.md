# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to fostering an open, welcoming, and respectful community.
All participants in this project—including maintainers, contributors, and users—
are expected to interact in a professional and constructive manner.

This Code of Conduct applies to all interactions within the project's repositories,
issue trackers, discussions, and other communication channels.

---

## Expected Behavior

Participants are expected to:

- Be respectful and considerate of differing viewpoints
- Provide constructive feedback
- Communicate professionally and respectfully
- Focus on what is best for the community and the project
- Show empathy toward other contributors

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation
- Personal attacks or derogatory comments
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Disruptive or hostile behavior that harms collaboration

---

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct.
Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, or other contributions
that violate these guidelines.

---

## Reporting Violations

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct,
please report it by contacting:

security@seon.io

All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled confidentially where possible.

---

## Enforcement Actions

Maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including:

- Warning the contributor
- Temporarily restricting participation
- Removing contributions
- Banning the contributor from the project
